About Us

Learn More About Us

Green wood Syria is a Modern design studio located in Damascus, Syria. It offers proposals for architectural, interior and garden design. Our goal is to meet the desires of our customers by creating unique designs that fit the high-end lifestyle and fit the living styles you desire and according to the required budget

Since our inception, thanks to God, we have successfully accomplished many projects

  • Middle East: Syria, Lebanon, Dubai, Qatar, ...
  • Europe: Germany, Netherlands and Turkey

Green Wood is a partnership of expertise of real estate institutions that has a well-known name in the real estate market and aims to provide suitable housing solutions for all to relieve them of the burden of thinking about owning homes with which their dreams will come true and meet their aspirations in the shortest, most flexible and flexible way of ownership and investment.
